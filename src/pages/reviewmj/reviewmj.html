Ian Somerhalder

January 10, 2016

"Bad" is a fair-to-strong array of soul and rock blends. If the aims are modest, there's nothing low-key about the way producer Quincy Jones assembles a track, and his sound is typically forceful, vivid, rich and deep. It also tends to be a little mechanical--especially the way he splices in Jackson's tightly woven multi-tracked backing vocals.
